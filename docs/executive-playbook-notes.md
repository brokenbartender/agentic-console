# Agentic AI Executive Playbook Notes

Source: agentic-ai-the-new-frontier-in-genai-an-executive-playbook.pdf

Key takeaways:
- Agentic AI defined by autonomy, goal orientation, and environment interaction.
- Evolution from rule-based systems to multimodal agents (text/image/audio).
- Emphasis on operational readiness, safety, and governance for enterprise adoption.
- Highlights the need for autonomy controls and maturity progression.

Applied to this app:
- Added autonomy levels (supervised, semi, autonomous) with confirmation policy.
- Added model routing and multimodal hooks as building blocks.
- Strengthened traceability and operational docs.
